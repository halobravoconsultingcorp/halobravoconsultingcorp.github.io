🌿 A Sacred Path of Cleansing, Protection, and Renewal

⚠️ Safety First

Always burn your candle on a stable, heat-resistant surface. Keep away from flammable items, pets, and children. Never leave a burning candle unattended. If your ritual involves fire or smoke, ensure proper ventilation and keep water nearby just in case. 🔥🕊️

🕯️ Cleansing the Candle

Your White Sage Ritual Candle from Halo Publishing is delivered fully pre-cleansed and energetically blessed. It is ready to use the moment you unwrap it.

Still, you may choose to perform a cleansing ritual if the candle has been used previously, exposed to intense emotional energy, or if you simply desire a deeper spiritual bond before working your magick.

  • White sage is naturally purifying—its energy is infused directly into the candle.
  • This candle can even be used to cleanse other tools, such as crystals, tarot decks, or ritual spaces.

Optional ways to cleanse your candle:

  • 🌬️ Smoke Cleansing: Gently pass the unlit candle through sage or palo santo smoke.
  • 🌕 Moonlight Bath: Place it under moonlight to refresh and awaken its energy.
  • 🔔 Sound Cleansing: Ring a bell or use soft chimes to dispel residue from its energy field.
  • 🧂 Salt Circle: Surround it with sea salt for a purifying earth-aligned cleanse.

✨ All Halo Publishing products are pre-cleansed with care. These rituals are optional and meant to personalize your practice.

🔮 Spell 1: Cleanse a Room of Negative Energy

Purpose: To clear heavy or stagnant energy from a physical space.

  1. Place your candle near the center of the room or by the door.
  2. Light it and say:
    “With this flame, I cleanse and release all energies not aligned with peace.”
  3. Walk clockwise around the room slowly, visualizing a soft sage-colored light clearing the air.
  4. If desired, gently ring a bell or bowl to amplify the clearing.
  5. Return to the candle and snuff the flame with gratitude.

✂️ Spell 2: Cord-Cutting & Emotional Release

Purpose: To energetically detach from someone or something you’re ready to let go of.

  1. Write what you wish to release on a small piece of parchment.
  2. Light your White Sage Candle and say:
    “With this flame, I free myself from what weighs me down.”
  3. Very carefully, burn the parchment in a fireproof bowl.
  4. As it turns to ash, imagine the emotional cords dissolving.
  5. Snuff the candle and bury or scatter the cooled ashes outdoors.

🪶 Spell 3: Aura Dusting Ritual

Purpose: To cleanse your energy field of stress, clutter, and energetic residue.

  1. Light the candle in a quiet space.
  2. Take a clean feather (or fresh sprig of rosemary or lavender) and gently sweep around your body from head to feet.
  3. Repeat softly:
    “I brush away all that does not belong to me.”
  4. Visualize the candle drawing out tension and sending it to the earth.
  5. Snuff the flame when done and take a deep, cleansing breath.

💎 Spell 4: Charging Sacred Tools

Purpose: To infuse an object with fresh energy and protective light.

  1. Place the item (e.g., a crystal or charm) near the burning candle.
  2. Say:
    “May this object carry clarity, peace, and sacred intention.”
  3. Close your eyes and imagine light from the candle flowing into the item.
  4. Let it sit in the candle’s glow for 5–10 minutes, then snuff the candle.

🛡️ Spell 5: Energetic Protection

Purpose: To surround yourself with a shield of calm and sacred protection.

  1. Light your White Sage Candle and sit with a bowl of water beside you.
  2. Say aloud:
    “With sacred flame and sage’s breath, I am shielded from harm, from now until next.”
  3. Visualize a glowing light encircling your body.
  4. Dip your fingers into the water and touch your forehead, chest, and hands to anchor the spell.
  5. Snuff the flame to seal the energy.
