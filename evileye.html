Guide to Your Evil Eye Protection Candle

Manifest protection, radiate light, and shield your spirit.

About Your Candle

Your Halo Publishing evil eye protection candle is crafted with care and pre-cleansed using sacred rituals. Though additional cleansing isn’t required, performing a personal ritual can help you feel more connected to its energy.

How to Cleanse Your Candle

Option 1: Smoke Cleansing

Burn cleansing herbs like sage, palo santo, or rosemary. Allow the smoke to encircle your candle, imagining it absorbing protective energy.

Option 2: Full Moon Charge

Leave your candle under the full moon overnight to charge it with lunar energy, perfect for enhancing its protective powers.

Option 3: Intention Setting

Hold your candle, breathe deeply, and focus on your intention. Imagine a bright, protective light surrounding it, ready to shield you from negativity.

Spells for Your Candle

Spell 1: Aura Shield

Purpose: To create a protective aura around you.

Instructions:

  1. Light the candle and gaze into the flame.
  2. Recite:
    “O flame of might, burning high,
    Wrap me in light, beneath the sky.
    No ill shall pass, no harm shall stay,
    I am protected, night and day.”
  3. Visualize a glowing shield around your body, shimmering in deep blue and white light.

Spell 2: Evil Eye Deflector

Purpose: To reflect negative energy and jealousy.

Instructions:

  1. Light the candle and take three deep breaths.
  2. Recite:
    “Candle flame, so strong and bright,
    Reflect ill will, return the blight.
    Let kindness come, let darkness flee,
    As I will, so mote it be.”
  3. Envision negativity being reflected away from you, replaced by calming light.

Spell 3: Path of Wisdom

Purpose: To gain insight and clarity in difficult times.

Instructions:

  1. Light the candle and close your eyes.
  2. Recite:
    “Flame of wisdom, pure and true,
    Guide my steps in all I do.
    Bring clarity and light my way,
    Bless my journey, come what may.”
  3. Spend a few moments in quiet reflection, allowing new insights to emerge.

Candle Care Tips

  • Always trim the wick to 1/4 inch before lighting to ensure an even burn.
  • Allow the candle to burn for at least one hour on first use to prevent tunneling.
  • Keep the candle away from drafts and never leave it unattended while burning.
