Aromatherapy Blends

Relaxing Night Time Diffuser Blend

  • 4 drops Halo Naturals Lavender Essential Oil
  • 3 drops Cedarwood Essential Oil
  • Add to diffuser per 100ml of water

Uplifting Daytime Diffuser Blend

  • 3 drops Halo Naturals Lavender Essential Oil
  • 2 drops Sweet Orange Essential Oil
  • 2 drops Grapefruit Essential Oil
  • Add to diffuser per 100ml of water

Skincare Recipes

Lavender Face Serum

  • 2 tablespoons jojoba oil
  • 5 drops Halo Naturals Lavender Essential Oil
  • 3 drops rosehip oil
  • Mix and apply a few drops to face and neck after cleansing

Lavender Sugar Scrub

  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup coconut oil
  • 10 drops Halo Naturals Lavender Essential Oil
  • Mix and use to gently exfoliate skin in the shower

Home Care Solutions

Lavender All-Purpose Cleaner

  • 1 cup water
  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • 10 drops Halo Naturals Lavender Essential Oil
  • Combine in a spray bottle and use on surfaces

Lavender Air Freshener

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon vodka or rubbing alcohol
  • 15 drops Halo Naturals Lavender Essential Oil
  • Mix in a spray bottle and spritz to freshen the air
