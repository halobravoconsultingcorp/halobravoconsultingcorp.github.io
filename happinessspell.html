⚠️ Candle Safety Disclaimer

Please burn your candle on a fire-safe surface and never leave it unattended. Keep it away from flammable materials, children, and pets. If your ritual involves paper, herbs, or other flammable items, have a bowl of water nearby for safety. 🔥🍊

🌟 Candle Ingredients & Their Magical Meaning

  • Bergamot (scent): Bright citrus that lifts the mood, restores balance, and energizes the spirit. Bergamot clears emotional clouds and promotes confidence.
  • Orange Peel (herb): Sweetens energy, draws cheer and enthusiasm, and connects you to light-hearted creativity.
  • Citrine (crystal): A beacon of joy, citrine sparks laughter, courage, and radiant self-worth. It dissolves heaviness and encourages a sunny perspective.

This golden yellow candle is a spell of celebration and emotional freedom. Use it anytime you need a boost of light or a joyful realignment.

🧼 Cleansing & Charging the Candle

All Halo Publishing candles are pre-cleansed and lovingly prepared before shipment. However, you may add your own cleansing ritual to personalize your bond with the candle.

To cleanse:

  • 💨 Blow gently over the candle to remove stagnant energy.
  • 🔔 Ring a bell or clap three times over the candle with joyful intention.
  • 🌞 Place in sunlight for up to an hour to infuse radiant solar power.

To charge it:

  1. Hold the candle in both hands.
  2. Smile softly and say:
    “With this flame, I invite light. Joy fills me. Gratitude grounds me.”
  3. Light it and let the warm glow activate your spellwork.

🌻 Spell 1: Sunlight Manifestation

Purpose: To awaken optimism and clarity on days you feel heavy or blocked.

  1. Light your candle and sit where you can see natural light.
  2. Say:
    “As the sun rises, so do I. My spirit is light. My path is bright.”
  3. Visualize sunlight filling your chest with golden warmth and glowing outward.
  4. Snuff the candle and begin your day with a single positive action.

🎈 Spell 2: Release into Joy

Purpose: To let go of stress and welcome laughter and play.

  1. Write one thing that’s been weighing you down on a small piece of paper.
  2. Light the candle and say:
    “This weight is not mine to carry. I choose joy.”
  3. Tear the paper and throw it in the trash with intention—or burn it safely in a fireproof dish.
  4. Laugh out loud—fake it if needed—and feel your energy shift.

🍊 Spell 3: Citrus Grin Spell

Purpose: To boost mood and spark natural joy.

  1. Peel an orange while the candle is lit.
  2. As the citrus oil mists the air, say:
    “Sweet fruit, bright flame. Joy is simple. Joy is mine.”
  3. Eat the orange slowly, mindfully, and with gratitude.

💛 Spell 4: Radiance in the Mirror

Purpose: To increase self-love, confidence, and magnetic positivity.

  1. Place the candle near a mirror and light it.
  2. Look into your own eyes and say:
    “I radiate warmth. I am the light I seek.”
  3. Smile and hold your gaze. Let the flame reflect your inner glow.

📒 Spell 5: Golden Gratitude List

Purpose: To multiply joy through simple gratitude.

  1. Light the candle and open a notebook or blank sheet.
  2. Write down 5 things you're grateful for—no matter how small.
  3. After each item, say:
    “Thank you. More of this, please.”
  4. Snuff the candle and reread your list anytime you feel low.

💛 May joy return to you easily, abundantly, and often. Let the light in.
