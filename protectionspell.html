⚠️ Candle Safety Disclaimer

Always burn your candle on a heat-resistant surface, in a well-ventilated area. Never leave it unattended. If your ritual involves herbs, paper, or burning objects, keep a bowl of water or sand nearby for safety. 🔥🖤

🌲 Candle Ingredients & Their Meaning

  • Cedarwood (scent): Symbolizes strength, ancient wisdom, and protective grounding. Cedar is associated with ancestors, sacred groves, and endurance.
  • Obsidian (crystal): A powerful spiritual shield and truth mirror. Obsidian deflects negative energy and reveals hidden threats, both psychic and mundane.
  • Juniper berries (herb): Burned for centuries to cleanse spaces of spiritual harm. Juniper wards off envy, ill-wishes, and bad luck.

This black candle acts as a guardian, sealing your energy field, home, or altar space in protection and clarity.

🧼 Cleansing & Charging the Candle

All Halo Publishing candles are pre-cleansed and spiritually prepared for ritual use. However, you may wish to add your own energetic touch before working with the candle.

To cleanse:

  • 🧂 Surround it with a ring of salt or black tourmaline for 10 minutes.
  • 🔔 Ring a bell or clap your hands around the candle to disperse any lingering energy.
  • 🌙 Place it under moonlight—especially a waning or new moon—for an energetic reset.

To charge for protection:

  1. Hold the candle firmly in both hands.
  2. Visualize a cloak of darkness wrapping around it like armor.
  3. Say:
    “I call in the guardians. This flame is my shield. Let no harm cross its light.”

🚪 Spell 1: Threshold Warding Ritual

Purpose: To guard the front door of your home from spiritual intrusion.

  1. Place the candle just inside your front door.
  2. Light it and sprinkle a pinch of salt and crushed juniper berries in front of the door.
  3. Say:
    “All harm, stay back. All peace, come in. This door is watched by flame and will.”
  4. Let the candle burn for at least 11 minutes, then snuff it out.

🪞 Spell 2: Mirror Reversal Spell

Purpose: To reflect negative energy or ill intent away from you.

  1. Place a small mirror behind the candle, facing outward.
  2. Light the candle and say:
    “Return to sender what is not mine. This mirror speaks: not this time.”
  3. Visualize all malice bouncing off your field and dissolving into smoke.
  4. Snuff the candle. Cleanse the mirror later with water or moonlight.

✂️ Spell 3: Cord-Cutting for Protection

Purpose: To sever energetic ties with draining people or situations.

  1. Write the name of the person or source of harm on a slip of paper.
  2. Light the candle. Hold the paper near the flame (safely) and say:
    “I release this tie. My energy is mine.”
  3. Cut the paper with scissors and burn both halves in a fire-safe dish.
  4. Snuff the candle and dispose of the ashes outdoors or in running water.

🧿 Spell 4: Evil Eye Reversal

Purpose: To deflect envy, gossip, and energetic attacks from others.

  1. Draw an eye symbol on a bay leaf or small piece of paper.
  2. Light the candle and say:
    “What’s sent with harm returns with grace. The evil eye leaves no trace.”
  3. Burn the eye paper in a fireproof dish and visualize your aura clearing like smoke lifting.
  4. Snuff the candle. Optional: carry a piece of obsidian with you for extra defense.

🌙 Spell 5: Dream Shielding Spell

Purpose: To protect your sleep from nightmares, psychic attacks, or spiritual interference.

  1. Before bed, light the candle in your bedroom and hold a small obsidian or juniper berry.
  2. Say:
    “As I sleep, I am safe. No shadow shall enter my dream gate.”
  3. Place the stone near your pillow. Snuff the candle and go to bed calmly.
